Our Services

Welcome to JMOC

Driven by a vision for a cleaner future, recent high school graduate, Joe Salemi, established JMOC Services. Committed to bettering the community, our team, comprised of young individuals, offer residential garbage bin cleaning services, to guarantee fresh bins. Alongside garbage bin cleaning, JMOC offers pressure washing, snow removal, and landscaping to ensure your needs are met year round. We offer our services in Arlington Heights, Palatine, Glenview, Mount Prospect, Prospect Heights, and surrounding communities.
